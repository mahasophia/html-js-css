Spring Rolls Recipe ü•¢

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 8 spring roll wrappers
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix all veggies with soy sauce.
  2. Roll tightly in wrappers.
  3. Fry until crispy golden.
  4. Serve hot with sauce.

Bruschetta Recipe üçû

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • Fresh basil (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Toast bread slices.
  2. Mix tomato, garlic, oil, vinegar, basil.
  3. Top bread with mixture.
  4. Serve immediately.
