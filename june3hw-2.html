Chennai

Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant metropolitan city located on the Coromandel Coast off the Bay of Bengal. Known for its deep-rooted traditions and cultural heritage, Chennai is also a major IT and industrial hub. The city is home to Marina Beach, one of the longest urban beaches in the world, and renowned landmarks such as Fort St. George, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and San Thome Basilica. It also has a strong music and dance scene, especially the classical Carnatic music and Bharatanatyam. Chennai offers a perfect blend of the old and the new.
