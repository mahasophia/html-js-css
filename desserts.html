Chocolate Cake Recipe 🍫

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add butter, eggs, and milk, and blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased cake pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool and serve!

Ice Cream Sundae Recipe 🍦

Ingredients:
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped nuts
  • Cherry for topping
Instructions:
  1. Place ice cream scoops in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with chocolate syrup.
  3. Top with whipped cream and nuts.
  4. Garnish with a cherry and serve!

Fruit Tart Recipe 🥧

Ingredients:
  • 1 pre-made tart shell
  • 1 cup custard or cream filling
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup kiwi slices
  • 1 tbsp honey (for glaze)
Instructions:
  1. Fill tart shell with custard.
  2. Arrange fruit slices on top.
  3. Drizzle with honey for glaze.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.
