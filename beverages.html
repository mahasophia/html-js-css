Mango Smoothie Recipe üçπ

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup milk or almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Blend mango, milk, and honey until smooth.
  2. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve chilled!

Lemonade Recipe üçã

Ingredients:

  • 2 lemons (juiced)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix lemon juice and sugar in a pitcher.
  2. Add cold water and stir well.
  3. Serve over ice and garnish with mint leaves.

Cold Coffee Recipe ‚òï

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend milk, coffee powder, sugar, and ice cubes until frothy.
  2. Pour into a tall glass.
  3. Top with whipped cream if desired and serve cold.
