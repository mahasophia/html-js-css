Butter Chicken Recipe 🍗

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken with butter until golden brown.
  2. Add tomato puree, garam masala, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes, then stir in cream.
  4. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Paneer Tikka Recipe 🧀

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate paneer with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill or bake until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Grilled Fish Recipe 🐟

Ingredients:

  • 2 fish fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rub fish with oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  3. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve hot.
